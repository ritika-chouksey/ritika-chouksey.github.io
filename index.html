Introduction

I am a desecedent of American Slaves, problem solver in training, and second year PhD student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My advisor is Timothy Bretl, I am interested in priming robotic manipulation tasks with computer vision algorithms.

In the past, I was a Research Engineer Intern at Latent Space. During my undergraduate experience, I had the privelege of working with Daniel Ritchie at Brown Univeristy, Christoph Mertz at the Carnegie Mellon University Robotics Institute, and Bo Li at the University of Southern Mississippi.
